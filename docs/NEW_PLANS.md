# Payment and Plans

## Standard Plan - Auto Charge

When users exceed 100 minutes of video generation:
- **1,000 yen per 20 minutes** will be charged automatically
- **1 additional video & voice training** will be added per 1,000 yen auto charge

---

## Free Plan

| Feature | Limit |
|---------|-------|
| Training | 1 video and voice training |
| Video generation | 3 minutes |

### Concerns

- **Device restriction**: Need to think about device restriction to prevent users from creating many accounts with many emails
- **Account deletion abuse**: In case users delete accounts and try to recreate, we should keep some record (free plan usage and email) and prevent them from using free plan again
